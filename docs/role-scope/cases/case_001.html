Case #001 — Supply Contract Dispute

Award (Public)

Claim partially upheld; Respondent must pay arrears.

Reasons

[Arbitrators only — Full reasons]

Based on exhibits E-12 and E-19 and testimonies W-02/W-05, together with Article 7.2, the interruption cannot be attributed to the claimant. The “material adverse shift” clause requires ≥2/3 indices to exceed thresholds for two consecutive quarters; this was not proven by the respondent. Partial award is therefore granted.

[Parties — Key grounds summary]

  • “Material adverse shift” not satisfied (no proof).
  • Exhibits E-12/E-19 and testimonies W-02/W-05 accepted as credible.
  • Payment obligation confirmed but reduced.

Full reasons are available to parties in the official portal.
